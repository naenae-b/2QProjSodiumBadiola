Poverty in Depth

Poverty is geneeally defined as a state of living wherein on lacks the resources needed to accord the minimum necessities in life, such as food, shelter, clothing, and the like.

Homelessness in Depth

Homelessness is generally defined as the lack of a residence that is stable, safe, and permanent.
